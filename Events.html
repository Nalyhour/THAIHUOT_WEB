Pastry Hand-on Training Workshop

Special Events : 26th till 29th June 2023

Cambodia exclusive distributor Thai Huot Trading Co., Ltd. together with France Elle & Vire Professionel of Savencia Group held workshops on Pastry Hands-On Training to participants by exclusively invitations only from 26th till 29th June 2023 at NagaWorld Hotel Phnom Penh, Rosewood Hotel Phnom Penh, Dara Airport Hotel Phnom Penh and Don Bosco Hotel Sihanoukville. Pastry chefs or pâtissier in French, experienced hands-on sessions, techniques to improve and refine with Elle & Vire Professionnel Pastry Chef Jonathan Leger.

Cambodia exclusive distributor Thai Huot Trading Co., Ltd. together with France Elle & Vire Professionel of Savencia Group wish to express their gratitude to all the honorable participants for making the event a success!

Elle & Vire Pastry-hand on Training Workshop 2022

Special Events : 22 February 2022

Cambodia exclusive distributor Thai Huot Trading Co., Ltd. together with Elle & Vire Professionel of France, held workshops on Pastry Hands-On Training to participants by exclusively invitations only from 21st till 22nd February 2023 at From 21 to 22 February 2022 at Thai Huot Company, located at Lot 1565, Veng Sreng Blvd., Choam Chao Village, Choam Chao Sangkat, Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Pastry chefs or pâtissier in French, experienced hands-on sessions, techniques to improve and refine with Elle & Vire Professionnel Pastry Chef Jonathan Leger.

Jonathan Leger is a professional chef with extensive experience in baking and training of trainees from around the world, especially in Southern Asia. He previously worked as a chef for a three-star Michelin restaurant in Brussels, Belgium and is currently the head chef of Savencia.

Thai Huot Commercial Co., Ltd., together with Elle & Vire Professionel of France, would like to thank all the guests for taking the time to attend and make this event a success.

Thai Huot Donation to Don Bosco Hotel School

Special Events : 28th & 29th June 2023

Cambodia exclusive distributor Thai Huot Trading Co., Ltd. together with France Elle & Vire Professionel of Savencia Group held workshops on Pastry hands on training to Sihanoukville Don Bosco Hotel School on 28th & 29th June 2023.

Thai Huot Trading and Elle&Vire has sponsored Don Bosco Hotel School culinary students to join the Chef demo training and Thai Huot Trading also sponsored many F&B items to the School such as Elle&Vire Butter, Elle&Vire Whipping Cream, Elle&Vire Cooking Cream, Elle&Vire Yogurts, Elle&Vire Milk, Uni-President Flour, CAMPAGNA Pasta, GRANONO Pasta, ELITA Cooking Oil, KEAN Juice, NIKUDO Frozen Tim Sum & YOKI frozen Tim Sum.

Once again, Thai Huot Management would like to thank you Chef Jonathan Leger, exclusively invited guest, Chefs from various Hotel and Don Bosco Hotel School Management for making this successful event
